Hi. I'm Alex. I'm going to teach you a little about me.

First, let me say that I've been out of school for about 4 years now. After I graduated high school in Calgary, Alberta, I enrolled in the University of Calgary for Computer Science. That didn't work out for various reasons and I eventually went to the Southern Institute of Technology to learn how to be a Emergency Medical Responder. Even with the experience of these courses, I still wasn't sure what kind of career I wanted.

During my time at the University of Calgary I met my current girlfriend whom I eventually moved in with. This would be my first time living away from home. After a year in Calgary, we decided to move to Vancouver as we both had family here and missed living here. Upon arriving in Vancouver about a year ago, we both got new jobs to save money to afford schooling. I ended up working for a construction company. It paid fairly well and cemented my want for a non labor intensive career. Fast forward a bit and here I am. I believe IT work to be the career path I want and I'm going to try my very best to achieve it.
